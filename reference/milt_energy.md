# Synthetic hourly energy consumption with covariates

Hourly electricity consumption (MWh) for a single site over 60
consecutive days (1 440 observations), together with temperature and a
weekend indicator. Designed for covariate-aware forecasting examples.

## Usage

``` r
milt_energy
```

## Format

A
[`tibble::tibble()`](https://tibble.tidyverse.org/reference/tibble.html)
with 1 440 rows and 4 columns:

- datetime:

  `POSIXct` (UTC). Timestamp at the start of each hour.

- consumption:

  `numeric`. Energy consumed during the hour (MWh, non-negative).

- temperature:

  `numeric`. Ambient temperature in degrees Celsius.

- is_weekend:

  `integer`. `1` on Saturday or Sunday, `0` otherwise.

## Source

Synthetically generated by `data-raw/prepare_datasets.R`.
