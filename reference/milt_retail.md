# Synthetic multi-series monthly retail sales

Monthly sales figures (synthetic) for five product categories from
January 2010 to December 2022 (156 months each). Each series exhibits a
linear trend, monthly seasonality, and Gaussian noise, making this
dataset suitable for multi-series forecasting demonstrations.

## Usage

``` r
milt_retail
```

## Format

A
[`tibble::tibble()`](https://tibble.tidyverse.org/reference/tibble.html)
with 780 rows (5 categories \\\times\\ 156 months) and 3 columns:

- date:

  `Date`. First day of each month.

- category:

  `character`. One of `"Electronics"`, `"Clothing"`, `"Food"`,
  `"Furniture"`, `"Toys"`.

- sales:

  `numeric`. Monthly sales in arbitrary units (non-negative).

## Source

Synthetically generated by `data-raw/prepare_datasets.R`.
